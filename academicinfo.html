Incomplete Grades - back to top

Incomplete grades can be received at the end of each quarter for legitimate reasons as determined by the teacher or team.  Any student who does not have work completed will have ten school days to complete the work and turn it in to the teacher.  This does not apply to the last quarter of the school year.  Students need to have all their work turned in by the last day of school.  No incomplete will be given the fourth quarter.  When an incomplete is given and the work is turned in, the incomplete will turn into the appropriate letter grade.

Honor Roll - back to top

The "A" and "B" Honor Rolls are established after each quarter grading period.  Students who have a GPA of 3.7 or above are listed on the "A" Honor Roll and those with a GPA from 2.7 - 3.69 are listed on the "B" Honor Roll.  

GPA's are the average of all quarter point values (shown below).  An incomplete grade disqualifies the student for the Honor Roll.
